A solution for municipalities and urban utilities

The repair of sewer networks in the city is always a challenge: the need for approvals, access to private property, the discontent of residents and the endless restoration of landscaping. SP!RES completely changes this approach.

Our technology allows you to carry out sanitation remotely, without entering the territory of households and without opening the asphalt. The work is carried out from existing observation wells as quickly and unobtrusively as possible for the townspeople.
