Stages

Process technology: from the input to the finished pipe

01

Preparation and broaching

A special liner is placed inside the system and stretched in a straight line using a cable to the repair site.

02

Orientation and inversion

The device is inserted into the main channel and focuses on the problematic branch.

03

Polymerization

A liner is supplied through the hose. The calibration sleeve inflates, tightly pressing the new liner material against the walls of the old pipe.

04

Monitoring and completion

Built-in sensors monitor temperature and pressure in real time until a durable new pipe is formed inside the old one.
